The Best Type of Cookie

Many people prefer different cookies for various reasons. Over the years I have created and tried various cookies. I have created this post to share my favorite cookies and some recipes I have used to make them.

  • Chocolate Chip Cookie

    There are many reasons chocolate chip cookies are my favorite cookie and at the top of this list. The classic chocolate chip cookie has the perfect balance of sweetness and chocolate. The cookie is easy to make and is a great snack for any occasion. This cookie is a staple in my home.

    Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

  • White Chocolate Matcha Cookie

    As someone who loves a good matcha latte in the morning, I have grown to experiment with many different types of matcha desserts. I have found this cookie to be a good mix of matcha and cookie. Perfect for any matcha lover to try.

    Matcha Cookie Recipe

  • Brownie Cookie

    I love the decadent chocolate flavor of a brownie, and when that meets the perfect texture of a cookie, it is a perfect combination. I have tried many different types of brownie cookies and have found this one to be the best.
